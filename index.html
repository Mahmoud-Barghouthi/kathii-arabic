Tips for speaking faster
Speak even when wrong
A mistake said with confidence is worth more than a correct sentence never spoken. Say it — Mahmoud will help you fix it at the end.
Describe around missing words
Don't know the word? Describe it in Arabic: "الشيء اللي بتحطيه على الرأس" instead of "قبعة". This is real communication.
Voice notes between sessions
Send Mahmoud a 30-second voice note in Arabic every few days. Speaking outside sessions accelerates everything.
Write words you hear in Palestine
The moment you hear a new word, write it in your phone. Your notes app is your best vocabulary book.
Think in images, not German
Don't translate sentence by sentence from German. Think in pictures and feelings, then find Arabic words for them.
Bad days are part of learning
Sometimes your Arabic will feel worse than before. That means your brain is reorganising. Keep going — it always comes back stronger.
